Why Visit Our Molendinar Showroom

If you're searching for flooring on the Gold Coast and want to feel confident in your choice, visit the Evolved Floors showroom in Molendinar. We've created a calm, design-led space where you can compare colours, textures, and finishes properly, with expert guidance from a team that understands both style and performance.

Choosing flooring isn't just about picking a plank you like. The right product depends on your home, your lifestyle, and the conditions it needs to handle - including pets, kids, open-plan living, high-traffic zones, and the realities of coastal humidity. In the showroom, we'll help you narrow down options and explain the differences that matter, like wear layers, water resistance, acoustic comfort, underlay requirements, and installation methods.

Bring your inspiration photos, plans, or a rough idea of your style, and we'll help you match flooring to your cabinetry, paint colours, and overall palette. If you're working with a builder, architect, or designer, we can also support specifications and sample coordination.

Our Gold Coast Range Includes:

  • Hybrid flooring - durability and style for active homes
  • Luxury vinyl - realistic looks with superior performance
  • Engineered timber - authentic wood with coastal stability
  • Carpet and laminate options - comfort and value
  • Acoustic wall panels and feature finishes

Once you've shortlisted your favourites, we make the next steps simple: request an instant quote, book a site measure, and get clear, all-inclusive pricing with realistic lead times. If you want a better way to do flooring on the Gold Coast, expert-led advice, a curated selection, and a smooth process from selection to installation, we're here to help.
