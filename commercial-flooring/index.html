Commercial Flooring Products

Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP)

High-traffic durability with stunning aesthetic. Perfect for offices, retail, and hospitality environments. Wear-resistant, easy to maintain, cost-effective.

Engineered Timber

Premium reception and executive spaces. Authentic wood appearance with superior dimensional stability. Suitable for climate-controlled environments.

Sheet Vinyl

Seamless wet areas. Hygienic, waterproof, easy-to-clean. Ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, medical facilities, and food service areas.

Carpet

Acoustic comfort and professional appearance. Office environments, open-plan spaces, corporate facilities. Stain-resistant and durable options available.

Laminate

Cost-effective commercial solution. Retail environments, commercial spaces, and high-volume applications. Durable wear layer, easy maintenance.

Specialty Finishes

Custom solutions for unique requirements. Acoustic panels, feature finishes, and brand-specific applications. We handle specialty requests.
