About Evolved Floors

Family-owned flooring experts serving Queensland since 2014

Our Story

What started as a passion for quality flooring has evolved into a trusted name across South East Queensland and Northern NSW. We've built our reputation on craftsmanship, transparency, and putting our customers first.

We're a family-owned business with 45+ years of combined industry experience. That means when you work with us, you're not dealing with a faceless corporation. You're working with people who care about your project, understand flooring inside and out, and stand behind every installation.

Since 2014, we've been helping homeowners, builders, architects, and businesses find the right flooring solution. Whether it's a Gold Coast coastal home that needs moisture-resistant hybrid flooring, a Brisbane commercial fit-out, or a Byron Bay renovation where every design detail matters, we bring expertise, integrity, and a genuine partnership approach to every project.
