## Content Patterns & Observations

### Page Structure Pattern
Most product/location pages follow:
1. **Hero/Title** - location or product name
2. **Why Choose [Product/Location]** - benefit-driven copy
3. **Options/Range** - what's available
4. **How It Works** - service description (measure, quote, install)
5. **Pricing** - transparent (when shown)
6. **CTAs** - multiple (quote, measure, phone, showroom)

### Common CTAs
- 1300 45 11 32 (phone)
- Instant quote
- Site measure/booking
- Visit showroom
- Contact form

### Product Comparisons (from timber page)
Content answers:
- What is it? (solid vs engineered, pros/cons)
- Why choose it? (benefits relevant to Gold Coast climate)
- What options? (types, styles, tones, widths)
- How to install? (professional requirement)
- How to care? (maintenance schedule)
- How much? (pricing transparency)

### Location Customization
- **Gold Coast:** Showroom-focused, humidity/coastal angle, established market
- **Brisbane:** Mobile service (no showroom), growth market, corporate/trade angle
- **Byron Bay:** Design studio, coastal/lifestyle premium, new market, cork/specialty products
- All three emphasize climate suitability (humidity, coastal, lifestyle fit)

### Brand Voice
- Expert/knowledgeable (45+ years, deep product knowledge)
- Accessible (family-owned, not corporate)
- Transparent (clear pricing, realistic about tradeoffs)
- Helpful (educational, consultative, not pushy)
- Local (suburb names, climate-specific, lifestyle fit)
